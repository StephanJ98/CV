About me

I was born on Saint-Louis, Alsace, France of a French father and a Spanish mother. I grew up in Galicia, Spain. So I am trilingual French - Spanish - Galician.

Since I was little I liked everything related to technology, and as I grew older I became increasingly interested in the world of computing. So naturally I would orient myself to a Baccalaureate in the technology branch and then to a computer science career. And then I enrolled in the Higher School of Computer Engineering at the University of Vigo (Campus Ourense).

Also since I was little I liked sports, specifically cycling and Rugby. Which I practiced for a couple of years with Club de Rugby Zalaeta in the lower categories. Then I ended up taking me to another club where I spend much more time and some of my best moments, the Club de Rugby Arquitectura Técnica ( C.R.A.T ). And finally, due to my studies, I had to move to another city quite far away, which forced me to change team again, this time playing with him. Club Ourense Rugby and also representing the Ourense Campus and the University of Vigo in Rugby 7 at regional and national level.

Apart from this passion for technology and sports, I am a great lover of nature, which led me to become interested and cultivate bonsai. And to Asian culture, in this case I am interested in the history and culture of tea.
